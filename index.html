Bruno F. R. de Sousa

São José dos Campos - SP, Brazil

Aerospace Engineer with a proven record in satellite systems and space flight dynamics. Specialist in aviation safety and continued airworthiness. Former member of the Aerospace Systems Laboratory at Brazil’s National Institute for Space Research (INPE) and former part of the Attitude and Orbit Control Subsystem (AOCS) team at the ITA Space Center (CEI). Team leader in the hardware-in-the-loop attitude and orbit simulation project and Master's student at ITA.

Passionate about Guidance, Navigation & Control (GNC), electronics and the rapidly evolving field of nanosatellites. I am committed to advancing aeronautical and space-technology projects that bridge research and real-world missions.
